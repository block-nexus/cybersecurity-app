## How to Use

1. **Open the Application**: Simply open `index.html` in any modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)

2. **Navigate**: Use the top navigation menu to access:
   - **Home**: Overview and progress dashboard
   - **Lessons**: Browse and complete interactive lessons
   - **Practice Quiz**: Test your knowledge
   - **Quick Tips**: View essential cybersecurity tips

3. **Complete Lessons**: 
   - Click on any lesson card to open it
   - Read through the content
   - Click "Mark as Complete" when finished
   - Your progress is automatically saved

4. **Take the Quiz**:
   - Click "Start Quiz" to begin
   - Answer each question by clicking on your choice
   - View explanations after each answer
   - See your final score at the end
